What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. It encompasses machine learning, natural language processing, robotics, and more.

Applications of AI

Healthcare

AI helps in diagnosing diseases, personalizing treatments, and even assisting in surgeries.

Finance

AI powers fraud detection, algorithmic trading, and personalized financial planning tools.

Transportation

Self-driving cars and smart traffic management systems are made possible with AI.

The Future of AI

The future of AI is promising, with potential to enhance human capabilities, automate complex tasks, and solve global challenges. Ethical use and regulation will play a crucial role in shaping its impact.
